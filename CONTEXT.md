# Rastro

Rastro is a community pet-recovery context. It defines the language for reporting, discovering, and resolving pet cases without treating animals as marketplace goods.

## Language

**Lost-Pet Recovery Network**:
A community service for reuniting missing pets with their caretakers through reports, sightings, alerts, and safe contact.
_Avoid_: Pet marketplace, pet sales platform

**Lost Pet Report**:
A report created by a caretaker when a pet is missing and community help is needed to find it.
_Avoid_: Missing pet post, lost pet listing

**Pet Profile**:
A member-owned record of a pet that can be reused across reports and listings.
_Avoid_: Pet account, pet post

**Caretaker**:
The member responsible for a pet profile, report, or listing.
_Avoid_: Owner, seller

**Found Pet Report**:
A report created when a person has found or secured a pet and is trying to identify or contact its caretaker.
_Avoid_: Found pet post, found pet listing

**Sighting Report**:
A report created when a person saw a pet but did not secure it.
_Avoid_: Found pet, sighting post

**Active Report**:
A report that is still relevant for discovery, contact, and nearby alerts.
_Avoid_: Open post, live listing

**Closed Report**:
A report that has reached an outcome and no longer triggers nearby alerts.
_Avoid_: Deleted report, archived post

**Report Outcome**:
The final or current resolution state of a report, such as still missing, reunited, transferred to shelter, unable to locate, or inactive.
_Avoid_: Status, report state

**Adoption Listing**:
A listing created by a member or organization to find a new caretaker for a pet without operating as a sale.
_Avoid_: Pet sale, buy pet, breeder listing

**Rehoming Listing**:
A future, moderated listing for a private caretaker seeking a new home for a pet without operating as a pet sale.
_Avoid_: Pet sale, free pet marketplace

**Visitor**:
A person using Rastro without signing in.
_Avoid_: Guest account, anonymous user

**Member**:
A signed-in person who can create reports, create adoption listings, manage their own activity, and choose alert preferences.
_Avoid_: Account, user

**Verified Organization**:
A shelter, rescue, veterinary clinic, or pet-service organization whose identity has been confirmed by Rastro.
_Avoid_: Official account, approved seller

**Verification Badge**:
A visible trust marker showing that a member represents a verified organization.
_Avoid_: Seller badge, premium badge

**Review Mode**:
A moderation state where new adoption listings require approval before becoming public, used when abuse risk is elevated.
_Avoid_: Marketplace approval, seller review

**Contact Option**:
A report-level choice that determines how others can contact the report owner, such as in-app chat, WhatsApp, or both.
_Avoid_: Public contact, contact method

**In-App Chat**:
A Rastro conversation between members about a report or listing, with notifications for new messages.
_Avoid_: Comments, public chat

**WhatsApp Contact**:
A report-level contact option that opens WhatsApp using the phone number the report owner chose to expose.
_Avoid_: Public phone number, direct message

**Exact Location**:
The precise location selected for a report, visible only where the report owner has allowed precise sharing.
_Avoid_: Public pin, exact address

**Approximate Location**:
A public location representation that helps nearby people understand the area without exposing an exact coordinate by default.
_Avoid_: Hidden location, vague location

**Location Cell**:
A coarse geographic area used for approximate public display, broad filtering, and privacy-preserving location grouping.
_Avoid_: Exact geohash, block

**Dynamic Alert Area**:
The area around a member's current or last detected location used to find nearby lost-pet reports and local pet resources.
_Avoid_: Saved search area, static alert zone

**Alert Radius**:
The distance around a dynamic alert area that a member chooses for nearby alerts and discovery.
_Avoid_: Search distance, notification distance

**Alert Subscription**:
A member's opt-in preference to receive notifications for new matching lost-pet reports near their dynamic alert area.
_Avoid_: Push campaign, notification blast

**Local Sponsor Placement**:
A clearly labeled paid placement for a local pet-related organization or business that appears in resource surfaces without affecting recovery priority.
_Avoid_: Ad boost, promoted pet, paid ranking

**Resource Provider**:
A veterinary clinic, shelter, groomer, pet food provider, trainer, or pet-related business that can appear as a local resource.
_Avoid_: Advertiser, seller

**Recovery Priority**:
The rule that lost, found, and sighting reports remain the primary content and are not outranked by paid placements.
_Avoid_: Sponsored ranking, paid priority
